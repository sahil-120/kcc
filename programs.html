JUNIOR ACADEMY (6 -10Years)

Our Junior Cricket Academy is perfect for the beginners (as well as more experienced new players) looking to move onto hard ball cricket. We introduce and enhance the fundamental skills of batting, bowling, fielding, wicket keeping and game play. We create more awareness about the game and ensure they enjoy and enhance their game. We undertake various measures to develop what it takes to be the best in modern-day cricket. We feel the game is about expressing in the middle and we put them through this process.

SENIOR ACADEMY (11 – 16Years)

Our Senior Crickets Academy helps to take the experienced player’s game to a new level, through a blend of one-to-one coaching, master classes, small group activities, video analysis, fitness conditioning and pressure training. Here we push the player to become an athlete as well as a professional. Fitness, matches, work ethics, and regime – these are the few vital ingredients that we add in creating a finished product. We push the individual physically and mentally in our quest to go the next level. Commitment and discipline are two words that we drill into the student’s mind.

FLOODLIGHT CRICKET TRAINING

The Khutwa-Cricket also offers Cricket Practice sessions under lights. The facility is available on hire to Clubs, Teams, Individuals, Groups, Communities. We have as many as 5 Season Ball Cricket Nets surrounded by 24 lights. The timings for booking nets under lights is 6:30 pm to 10:00 pm. For more details on the same and to book a slot kindly click on BOOK A SLOT tab in the Drop-Down Menu.

BOX CRICKET

At the Khutwa-Cricket, we also have BOX Cricket Facility open to all. The space can be used to play Under-arm and Over-arm Tennis ball cricket matches. It is a huge space covered with nets from all sides. Tennis ball Cricket can be played during the day and at nights as well. The cut-off time for Box Cricket is 10:00 pm on all days. For more details on the same and to book a slot kindly click on BOOK A SLOT tab in the Drop-Down Menu.
